Course: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (CS101)
Instructor: Dr. Alan Turing
Semester: Fall 2025

Course Description:
This course introduces the basic principles and applications of Artificial Intelligence. Topics include search algorithms, logic, machine learning, and ethics in AI.
